Blood Sugar Level


Diabetes is a chronic condition that affects how your body processes glucose (sugar). It’s essential to manage blood sugar levels effectively to prevent complications. Let’s explore the key aspects of diabetes.When your blood sugar levels fall within the recommended ranges, it’s a sign your diabetes treatment and management plan is working. You and your doctor can determine your exact blood sugar targets. Meanwhile, these general guidelines can help. Diabetes impacts your body’s ability to control your blood sugar (glucose) levels. Uncontrolled blood sugar can lead to serious complications, which is why monitoring your blood sugar and keeping it within recommended ranges is so important. Your recommended blood sugar range can be impacted by a variety of factors such as your age, overall health, and diabetes management goals.
